Palm Piano
Description

Ever felt like learning Piano or sightreading? Have a tune in your head that you want to jot down? Palm Piano can help you do both! It is not only an educational rhythm game for your Android device, you can also import custom MIDIs, practice with sheet music, and compose your own tracks to share with friends!
